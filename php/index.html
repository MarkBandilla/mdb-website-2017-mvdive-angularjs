Welcome to marco vincent divers
Experience the best of philippine diving

Marco Vincent Dive Center 1 is located on White Beach, Puerto Galera, Philippines. Rinse tubs, showers and other facilities for your comfort are just a few steps away from the crystal clear waters of the cove. Experienced and hard core divers will appreciate our state of the art scuba dive boats and cruisers. Our seasoned scuba dive pros will ensure your hassle free and safe scuba diving holiday.

Centrally located , we serve divers from the different resorts along White Beach.

Boasting top-notch classrooms and a training pool, the Marco Vincent Dive Center 2 is where we offer PADI Open water to Dive Master certifications. It is located next to our own Marco Vincent Dive Resort, a hundred meters inland, a welcome oasis after a busy day. Learn about your next adventure, explore and experience with a PADI 5 star Dive Center.

Whatever your budget, we are your one stop shop for your scuba diving holiday. From airport transfers, scuba dive packages and a diverse range of accommodations, we will be happy to be of service.
